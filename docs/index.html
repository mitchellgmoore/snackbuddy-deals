About SnackBuddy

SnackBuddy started after I became a dad, money got tighter, and I tried to get healthier at the same time.

When you’re trying to eat better, snacks matter. They can either quietly wreck a calorie deficit or help you hit goals like protein and fiber without much effort. I found myself buying the same products over and over, and I started noticing something: one week they’d be on sale, the next week they wouldn’t. After a while, paying full price started to feel like I was overpaying for things I bought all the time.

So I built SnackBuddy to do one simple thing: track the snacks and drinks people already buy and call out real price drops — so you can stock up at the right time or try something new when it’s cheaper.

How SnackBuddy Works

SnackBuddy tracks prices for a growing list of better-for-you snacks and drinks across major retailers.

At its core, it’s pretty simple: we keep an eye on specific product pages and pay attention when prices move. When something drops, it shows up here so more people can take advantage of it — without having to constantly check themselves.

Don’t see a product or retailer you want tracked yet? Let me know — the list is expanding over time.

Note: Prices and availability can vary by location, especially for in-store items.

How We Pick Snacks

Not everything that calls itself healthy earns a spot here. Every product is manually reviewed — we're looking for real protein content relative to calories, low added sugar, and meaningful fiber where it applies.

We're not here to be perfect. SnackBuddy isn't about finding the most aggressive diet food on the shelf. It's about finding things that are genuinely better than the default — snacks people actually want to eat, at a price worth buying them.

Don't see something you think belongs here? Let us know — the list is always growing.
